Baking a Banana Loaf Recipe with Kids

Another reason we love our banana loaf recipe here at www.BananaLoafRecipe.co.uk is that it is a great recipe to do with children. When you start cooking with kids you want things to do with them that are easy. The recipe needs to be fairly forgiving to be able to absorb those inevitable mistakes that occur from little accidents of spillage or over-enthusiastic mixing. You need to feel that they will be safe cooking with you yet you want them to join in as much as possible with the actual cooking - it’s not much fun for them if all they do is watch you press buttons on the food processor! Finally, you want to cook things for them that they actually like!

Baking a cake with kids is perfect on that front but most cake recipes involve creaming butter with sugar or melting butter - neither of which kids can actually do with ease. The banana loaf recipe we have here requires no electric mixers, no hard beating that needs strong arms and no saucepans on a hob. Banana loaf is a relatively healthy cake too so they can pig out a bit on the fruits of their labour and you won’t mind too much. Yes, it can be a bit messy doing it with kids but if you plan for that, you can all have fun together. We’ve cooked it with our children from the age of 2!
