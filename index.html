Banana Loaf Recipe

Welcome to the Banana Loaf Recipe website, the only website dedicated purely to that simple and most versatile of cakes, the banana loaf cake. Here you will find our definitive banana loaf recipe along with our tips about how to make your own banana loaf cake taste great.

View our various articles to learn about the ingredients to use, the equipment you might need and other handy hints to make your baking life easier. Most importantly, though, here is our banana loaf recipe. For a printable version, please click here.

Ingredients

8oz/225g wholemeal plain flour
4tsp baking powder
4oz/100g dark brown sugar
4oz/100g butter or margarine
1lb/450g bananas, mashed with a fork (approx 3-4; weight is with skin)
4oz/100g raisins
2 eggs, beaten

Equipment: a 2lb loaf tin (approx 23cm x 13cm x 7cm)

Method

1. Preheat the oven to 160 degrees celsius (for fan-assisted ovens use 150 degrees)

2. Prepare the loaf tin by lining with greaseproof paper

3. In a large mixing bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder and sugar. Stir well to break up any sugar lumps.

4. Add your butter or margerine to the dry ingredients. Rub the fat into the flour using the rubbing-in method. Click here for help with this. The result should be a breadcrumb-like texture.

5. Add the raisins, the mashed banana and the beaten egg.

6. Mix well untill there are no flour lumps. It should look like a thick batter.

7. Spoon the contents into the prepared loaf tin

8. Bake on the middle shelf of the preheated oven for 1 hour.

9. After 1 hour check your cake. The top should have risen and a skewer inserted into the cake should come out clean. Cracking of the top is a good sign.

10. Cool on a wire rack.

11. Slice and serve with or without butter.

12. Will keep for several days in an airtight tin.
